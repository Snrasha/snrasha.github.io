Edit Heroes



  • Load your mod, will load every skills, heroes. Will ignore the rest.
  • In the left:
  • In the search bar, you can search your skill. Write "Attack" for see every related skill about it. Or directly the name of the skill.
  • Click on any skill for display the description
  • In the middle:
  • Click on a skill slot. Then click on the skill on the left list for replace it
  • Click again on a skill slot for lost focus
  • Click on the left skill than you want add. Then click on a skill slot
  • Any duplicate will be red.
  • In the Bottom:
  • You can choose the hero than you want modify.
  • Set the name, unit, class (for neutral hero)
  • Download the modified hero.
